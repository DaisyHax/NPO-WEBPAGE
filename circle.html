    OUR CIRCLE

    We want to be a part of the circular economy and create business opportunities together with our partners. It is important for us that we operate in an industry that we are proud of. We make it possible to turn waste into energy and we connect buyers and sellers of waste products, but we want to do more. Reducing the input of raw materials is important for a sustainable future.

    Our ambition is to help our partners create their own paths in the circular economy in order to make sure that no resource is wasted and landfill is no option. However, we are not there yet but we are committed to find new ways of contributing to a better environment. Our business opportunities should not only give us positive outcomes, but result in positive effects for the environment as well. We want you to join our circle of partners so we can continue and develop the circular economy.
